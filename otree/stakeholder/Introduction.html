You will then be asked to assess the company's risk by modeling the Altman Z-score - an established risk indicator - for the current year.

The study will proceed as follows:

  1. You see a fellow analyst's risk assessment from the previous year XX00.
  2. You learn about the company's stakeholder assessment initiative to boost financial performance.
  3. You have to make a risk assessment for the new year XX01.

The information provided in the case materials does not necessarily represent the information available to you when making an actual assessment of this type. For this study, please make your assessment solely based on the information provided in the case materials. There are no right or wrong answers.

As the task requires a high level of concentration, it is important that you complete it in one sitting without interruption.
